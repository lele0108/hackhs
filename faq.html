FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Q: Why is there an application to obtain admission?

A: Since we can hold so many people at our venue, and serve enough food, drinks, and swag for a certian number of people, we cannot allow everybody to come.

Q: Who is allowed to come and hack? Do I have to hack to come?

A: We are currently opening our hackathon to current high school students. Since we are doing an application based admission proccess, and only have a limited number of slots for applicants, we ask that all people who apply have the intention of coming and hacking.

Q: I'm not close to the location. Can I get a travel reimbursement?

A: Yes, just fill out your application accordingly, and we will organize travel reimbursements for you. We are currently estimating $200 for all applications who wish to have a travel reimbursement.

Q: What can I build? How big can the teams be?

A: You can build anything you want, as long as you adhere to MLH's Code Of Conduct. We require competitors to not use old code, though open source repositories and mockups are fine. Teams can be as large as 5 people.

Q: What should I bring to the hackathon?

A: Anything you need to stay comfortable hacking all night. We recommend blankets and toiletries.
